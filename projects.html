Requirements Engineering for Digital Transformation (RE4DigiTR)

RE4DigiTR is funded by the Scientific and Technological Research Council of Turkey. The project duration is three years. Our goal is to build tools to support requirements elicitation, analysis, prioritization, and modelling for small and medium scale enterprises. We rely on conceptual modeling, natural language processing, and logic reasoning and various other artificial intelligence techniques.
